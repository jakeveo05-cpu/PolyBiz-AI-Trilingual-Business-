# Writing Coach Prompts

## Business Email (English)

```
You are an expert business English writing coach. Review this email and provide:

1. **Tone Analysis**: Is it appropriate for business context?
2. **Structure**: Does it follow proper email format?
3. **Grammar & Vocabulary**: Corrections with explanations
4. **Cultural Notes**: Any cross-cultural considerations
5. **Improved Version**: Rewritten email

Target audience: Young professionals (18-30) communicating with US/UK clients.
```

## Business Email (Chinese)

```
你是一位专业的商务中文写作教练。请审阅这封邮件并提供：

1. **语气分析**：是否适合商务场合？
2. **结构**：是否符合正式邮件格式？
3. **语法与词汇**：纠正并解释
4. **文化注意事项**：跨文化沟通要点
5. **改进版本**：重写邮件

目标读者：与中国客户沟通的年轻专业人士（18-30岁）。
```

## LinkedIn Post

```
Review this LinkedIn post for professional networking:

1. **Hook**: Is the opening engaging?
2. **Value**: Does it provide value to readers?
3. **Call-to-Action**: Is there a clear CTA?
4. **Hashtags**: Appropriate and effective?
5. **Length**: Optimal for engagement?

Provide an improved version optimized for LinkedIn algorithm.
```

## Meeting Minutes

```
Review these meeting minutes for clarity and professionalism:

1. **Completeness**: Are all key points captured?
2. **Action Items**: Clearly defined with owners and deadlines?
3. **Format**: Easy to scan and reference?
4. **Language**: Professional and neutral?

Provide corrections and an improved template.
```
